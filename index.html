Enrollment Application

Thank you in advance for requesting an application to become a citizen of the United Cherokee Ani-Yun-Wiya Nation. Citizenship in the United Cherokee Ani-Yun-Wiya Nation is open to any person who can provide evidence of their Cherokee bloodline by substantiating their direct descendency from a person whose name appears on any federal official roll.
